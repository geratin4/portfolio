Mario and Luigi

Project Description


Mario and Luigi Pizzeria faced challenges due to the absence of modern systems for order management, leading to a chaotic workflow. To address this issue as a group project, we aimed to develop a solution. Our goal was to create a user-friendly ordering system for customers. Additionally, we aimed to integrate this order system with the kitchen system, incorporating a smart oven for more efficient operations.

My Role

As one of the Front End Developers of my team. I was the one in charge of doing the design lay-out and to implement it in HTML and CSS and use JavaScript for some functionalities.

Professional Skills

Future-oriented organization


For the this project, we established a project plan that served as a valuable tool for monitoring our progress and ensuring we stayed on schedule. This plan enabled us to track our advancements and identify any potential delays in the project timeline.


Project Plan

Investigative problem solving


For this project, we received collective feedback as a group rather than individual feedback. As a team, we consistently sought input from teachers to understand how we could enhance our website and to gauge our overall progress.


Feed Backs

Personal leadership


During this project, I encountered an issue with pushing to Git on the day before our deadline, resulting in the accidental deletion of over 30 files in the group project. Recognizing the mistake, I took responsibility for the error and worked promptly to fix the problem.

Targeted interaction


The main focus of the stakeholder was to incorporate modern technology into the pizzeria, aiming to enhance overall operations. Additionally, the stakeholder expressed an interest in incorporating some minor design elements in the ordering system.
