UniCooked

Project Description


As a team, we wanted to help students with their common problems. We noticed that many struggle with cooking, so we decided to create a simple solution called Unicooked.

My Role

In this project, my responsibility was focused on the front end. Specifically, I took charge of designing the equipment page, curating budget-friendly recipes, and creating the layout for presenting the recipes.

Professional Skills

Future-oriented organization


Similar to the pizzeria project, we also developed a project plan with defined deliverables for Unicooked. I believe this approach significantly contributed to keeping us on track and setting clear goals for the pace at which we wanted to advance our website.


Project Plan

Investigative problem solving


For this project, we received collective feedback as a group rather than individual feedback. As a team, we consistently sought input from teachers to understand how we could enhance our website and to gauge our overall progress.

Trends and developments


For this project, we dedicated time to crafting a well-thought-out design. We conducted research on the layout of existing recipe websites, identifying required features and optimal color schemes.

Iterative process


Given that Unicooked is a website for students, we actively sought their feedback. Utilizing this information, we optimized the website categories to be as efficient and user-friendly as possible for the student audience.

Interactive prototypes


In shaping the layout and navigation of the website, we actively involved students for feedback. We created a paper prototype for them to interact with. Based on the received feedback, we incorporated necessary adjustments into our design.

target group


Our primary target audience for Unicooked was students. When developing the design and layout, as well as determining the essential recipe categories, we kept the needs and preferences of students in mind. This user-focus approach aimed to create a platform that is particularly well-suited and appealing to the student demographic.
